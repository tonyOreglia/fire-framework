The Four Elements of FIRE

A comprehensive methodology that transforms disorganized thoughts into structured insights and actionable wisdom

F

Flow

A daily practice of unfiltered, stream-of-consciousness writing for 15-20 minutes. This creates space for authentic self-expression without judgment or editing.

Daily practice
I

Index

A systematic tagging and categorization system that connects related themes across entries, creating a searchable knowledge repository that reveals patterns over time.

Pattern recognition
R

Reflect

A structured monthly review process that examines entries holistically across key life domains, surfacing insights that might otherwise remain hidden.

Monthly review
E

Extract

A dedicated repository for preserving your most valuable insights, creating a personal wisdom archive that grows more valuable and applicable over time.

Wisdom collection
