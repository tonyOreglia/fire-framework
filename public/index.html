The Four Elements of FIRE

A comprehensive methodology that transforms disorganized thoughts into structured insights and actionable wisdom

F

Flow

A daily practice of unfiltered, stream-of-consciousness writing for 20 minutes. This creates space for authentic self-expression without judgment or editing.

Daily practice
I

Index

Semantic tagging system that connects related themes across entries. Every tag becomes a future access point. This creates a searchable framework for revisiting ideas and moments.

Pattern recognition
R

Reflect

A structured monthly review process that examines entries holistically across key life domains, surfacing insights that might otherwise remain hidden.

Personal Growth Vectors
E

Extract

A searchable repository for preserving your most valuable insights, creating a personal wisdom archive that grows more valuable and applicable over time.

Wisdom collection
